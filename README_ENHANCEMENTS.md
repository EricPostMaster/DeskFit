# DeskFit: Enhancement Ideas

Below are enhancement ideas for future versions of DeskFit, beyond the MVP.

## User Engagement
- Gamification: Badges, streaks, and leaderboards.
- Customizable exercises and routines.
- Real-time encouragement and feedback.

## Exercise Detection & Feedback
- Support for multiple exercises (e.g., squats, stretches, neck rolls).
- AI-based feedback on exercise form and posture.
- Adaptive difficulty based on user performance.

## Reminders & Scheduling
- Smart reminders based on inactivity (detecting lack of movement).
- Integration with Google Calendar, Outlook, or other scheduling tools.
- Personalized scheduling based on user preferences or work hours.

## Privacy & Accessibility
- Enhanced privacy controls and transparency.
- Voice prompts and notifications.
- Alternative exercises for users with limited mobility.

## Analytics & Social Features
- Activity history and progress tracking dashboard.
- Social features: challenge friends, team goals, or group sessions.
- Integration with wearable devices (e.g., Fitbit, Apple Watch).

## Packaging & Platform
- Native desktop app (Electron) for better system integration.
- Mobile app version for on-the-go reminders and tracking.

---

These ideas can be referenced for future planning and prioritization.
